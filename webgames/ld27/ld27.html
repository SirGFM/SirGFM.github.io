Space Rift

Ludum Dare

Move with arrow keys

Press X to shoot

Press C to use the special (makes you invincible)

The special may only be used if it's not blinking

If you keep shooting for too long, the ship will overheat and won't be able to shoot for a while

Enemies may drop collectables

Loneliness

Ludum Dare

The player moves toward the mouse

Touch floating balls to befriend them

Befriended balls fly toward the player

The (broken) bar at the top represents the player's loneliness. If it get to 0, the game ends

Touching a red kitten makes all balls fly away and shortens the loneliness bar

Laser dude vs His enemies

DON'T! Seriously... this is bad... But, if you must...

Arrow keys to move

Hold shift down to move slowly

Press Z to shoot

Press X to use a bomb (clears nearby bullets)

Grab the thunders* that enemies drop (when killed) to increase your power

You are only killed if the center of your belt is hit

You may graze bullets for extra points

You get an extra life every 10000 points (or 100000, not sure...)

Controls may be remapped to any key

* No, those aren't bananas...
